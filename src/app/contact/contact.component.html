INTRODUCTION

The company Nautical Providers, S.A. de C.V. is a company founded with the purpose of offering provisioning, supply, and crew transportation services. Mainly within the Port of Lázaro Cárdenas, in terminals and on docked ships.

SPECIFIC GOAL

The purpose of the Company will be: 1. - The purchase, sale, import, and export of fruits, vegetables, cereals, livestock, vehicles, clothing, textiles, electronics, and all kinds of products similar to the above. 2. - The supply and provision of services to ships. 3. - Collection, transportation, storage, processing, treatment of non-hazardous waste and hazardous waste; Environmental cleaning in private and government companies, federal freight transportation service in the modality of specialized cargo service of residual waste materials, waste, or others.

MISSION

Provide high-quality materials and services at competitive prices, always seeking the complete satisfaction of our customers.
